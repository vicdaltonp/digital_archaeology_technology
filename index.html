Technology Today

Currently, society is in a state of unprecedented technological advancement. As a result, archaeology has been faced with new technologies and technique that have transformed the discipline.

In UVic's Anthropology 485 curriculum, four components of Digital Archaeology were explored:

  • Web Platforms
  • Vision and Visualisation
  • Digital Materiality and Space
  • Augemented Reality and Visual Materiality

Each of these sections focus on a number of technologies that have been implemented in Digital Archaeology. By using these topics as examples, one can begin to explore the current state of technology in Digital Archaeology.

Web Platforms

Web Platforms are an increasingly prominent way in which Digital Archaeology is developing.

Vision and Visualisation

Vision and Visualisation have been an important part of archaeology, and it has increasingly been a driving force of Digital Archaeology.

Digital Materiality and Space

Organizing data is an important part of archaeology, resulting in Digital Archaeology's exploration into the realm of Digital Materiality and Space.

Augmented Reality and Visual Materiality

Arguably the most forward-looking component of Digital Archaeology, Augemented Reality and Visual Materiality has much potential for the future of this developing discipline.
